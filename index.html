Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel outside the city for weddings?

Yes! We cover destination weddings across India. Travel and stay charges will be extra as per actuals.

How many days for photo delivery?

We provide 10-20 pics within 1 week for your social media. The full album and videos usually take 3-4 weeks.

What is the booking advance?

25% on booking, 50% when the program starts, and the remaining 25% on final delivery.
